Privacy Policy

Last updated 20 July 2026

The short version

DevDiffy is a free set of browser-based developer tools — a JSON formatter and a text/diff comparer. There's no account, no signup, and no backend. Whatever you paste into the JSON Formatter or Text Compare tools is processed entirely on your own device and is never uploaded, transmitted, or stored on any server.

The content you paste

All formatting, validation, and diffing happens locally in your browser using JavaScript that runs on your device. DevDiffy has no server-side component that receives, logs, or stores the JSON or text you work with — closing the tab clears it from memory.

The JSON Formatter's History feature is the one exception worth calling out: it saves your last 5 valid entries to your browser's own localStorage, on your device only. DevDiffy cannot read or access this data, and it's removed if you clear your browser's site storage for DevDiffy.

Cookies & analytics

DevDiffy does not use cookies, and it does not run any analytics or advertising trackers.

Third-party resources

Page fonts (Space Grotesk, JetBrains Mono) are served from Google's font infrastructure (fonts.gstatic.com). Loading these font files may expose your IP address to Google in the same way requesting any file from a third-party server does; DevDiffy doesn't control or receive that data.

Links in the navigation and footer to GitHub, LinkedIn, X, and Medium take you to third-party sites with their own privacy policies.

Changes to this policy

If DevDiffy ever adds a feature that changes how data is handled, this page will be updated and the date above will change accordingly.
